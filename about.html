What is ECET?


The ECET, or Engineering Common Entrance Test, is an entrance exam in India for candidates seeking admission to engineering and pharmacy programs, particularly in the state of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. It's primarily aimed at diploma holders and students who have completed their 10+2 education, allowing them to pursue undergraduate courses in engineering.

The exam tests knowledge in subjects like Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry, and the scores are used for admissions into various engineering colleges.
