General Instructions



  1. Total duration of examination is 180 minutes.
  2. The clock will set at the server.The countdown timer in the top right corner of screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the examination.When the timer reaches zero, the examination will end by itself.You will not required to end or submit your examination.
  3. The Question Palette displayed on the right side of screen will show the status of each question using one of the following symbols:

    • 1
      You have not visited / answered the question yet.

    • 1
      You have answered the question.

    • 1
      You have NOT answered the question, but have marked the question for review.


    4. Navigate to a Question


  4. To answer a question, do the following:

    1. Click on the question number in the Question Palette at the right of your screen to go to that numbered question directly. Note that using this option does NOT save your answer to the current question.
    2. Click on "Next" to save your answer for the current question and then go to the next question.
    3. Click on "Mark for Review & Next" to save your answer for the current question, mark it for review, and then go to the next question.

    5. Answering a Question


  5. Procedure for answering a multiple choice type question:

    1. To select your answer, click on the button of one of the options
    2. To change your chosen answer, click on the button of another option
    3. To save your answer, you MUST click on the "Next" button
    4. To mark the question for review, click on the "Mark for Review & Next" button.


  6. To change your answer to a question that has already been answered, first select that question for answering and then follow the procedure for answering that type of question.

    7. Navigating through sections


  7. Sections in this question paper are displayed on the top bar of the screen. Questions in a section can be viewed by clicking on the section name.
  8. After clicking the "Next button" on the last question for a section, you will automatically be taken to the first question of the next section.
  9. You can shuffle between sections and questions only After you submitted the last question of that section only
  10. Candidate can view the corresponding section summary as part of the legend that appears in every section above the question palette.
